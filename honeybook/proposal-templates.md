# HoneyBook Proposal Templates — Zuzu Collective

_One Smart File template per package. In HoneyBook, build each as a separate saved template._
_Before sending to a client, Zoe edits the personalized intro block and fills in the investment amount._

---

## How each template is structured

1. **Personalized intro** — Zoe writes this per client before sending (see starter language below)
2. **Scope of services** — pre-built, consistent per package
3. **Investment** — left blank in the template; Zoe fills in before sending
4. **Payment structure** — pre-built per package
5. **Next step** — brief CTA to sign and submit retainer

---

## Personalized intro — starter language

_Replace this entirely with something specific to the client's vision from the discovery call. 3–4 sentences max. Use their first name, reference something real from your conversation._

Example structure:
- Sentence 1: Acknowledge the call / express genuine enthusiasm for their specific event
- Sentence 2: Reference something specific they shared (the venue, the vibe, the detail that stood out)
- Sentence 3: Set up the proposal — "I put this together with [that vision] in mind"
- Sentence 4 (optional): Light CTA — "Take your time, and reach out with any questions"

Avoid: "your special day," "your big day," "bringing your vision to life," "excited for this journey"
Sign-off inside the proposal letter: **Sincerely,** (not "With love,")
